About Us

Wild Circus is a traditional circus company that offers visually captivating circus performances all around the world.

Performing under traditional European-style circus tents. Wild Circus is a "family" of 50 individuals which includes magicians, clowns, performers, coaches aswell as some of the wildest safari animals of the planet.

Come to laugh, dream and marvel at our Wild Circus show in a town or city near you.
